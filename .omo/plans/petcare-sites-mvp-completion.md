# petcare-sites-mvp-completion - Work Plan

## TL;DR (For humans)

**What you'll get:** A complete demonstrable PetCare vertical slice built around two Pico 2 W nodes. `entrance-01` publishes SHT31 and LD2410C readings; `petzone-01` publishes the same environment/presence readings plus two HX711 bowl weights and three raw FSR408 bed-pressure channels. The local backend calibrates the bed, fuses pressure with the PC webcam, records exactly `eating` and `resting` behaviors, persists exactly `no_meal_12h` and `bed_sensor_mismatch` warning anomalies, and drives one operational dashboard plus an owner-only Sites demo.

**Why this approach:** Backend-owned calibration keeps Pico firmware one-way and small: the nodes publish observations, while FastAPI owns the 60-second median baseline, polarity, hysteresis, camera fusion, rest sessions, the two explicit warning rules, nighttime exits, and seven-day comparison. Real PostgreSQL and authenticated Mosquitto remain the local-live proof, and raw home video stays local and ephemeral.

Physical Pico/sensor/webcam checks remain visibly `NOT RUN` until hardware is connected.

Reject payloads over 5 seconds future. **Local-day algorithm**

Use `ZoneInfo('Asia/Seoul')` for boundaries and store/query timestamps in UTC. Let `D` be today's local date: today is `[D midnight,now]`; baseline days are exactly full local days `D-7` through `D-1`, each converted to UTC. History is sufficient only if the earliest valid `petzone-01` pressure reading is at or before `D-7` midnight. Before then, `complete_days` is the count `0..6` of baseline days whose local start is at or after that earliest reading, status is `insufficient_data`, and baseline/difference/percent are null. Once sufficient, a day with no rest session contributes zero; sessions are clipped to every interval and an open session is clipped at `now` only for today. `baseline_seconds` is the half-up rounded integer mean of all seven daily totals. A zero mean returns `zero_baseline`, baseline `0`, difference `today_seconds`, percent null, and `complete_days=7`; otherwise return `ready`, difference `today-baseline`, percent `100*difference/baseline` rounded half up to one decimal, and `complete_days=7`. A nighttime exit is only a `pressure_exit` ending whose `Asia/Seoul` local date is `D` and time is `>=22:00` or `<06:00`.
