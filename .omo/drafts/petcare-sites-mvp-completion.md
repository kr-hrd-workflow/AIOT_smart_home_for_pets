## Exact warning rules

- `no_meal_12h`: dog/cat is ineligible while eating is open; otherwise use the latest closed eating row, exact `eligible_at=ended_at+12h`, and 15-minute dedupe.
- `bed_sensor_mismatch`: after 30 continuous seconds, `unconfirmed_pressure` is occupied pressure with no fresh dog or cat, has `subject_id=null`, and uses identity `petzone-01`; `sensor_check` is empty pressure with a selected dog/cat and uses that subject. Kind or subject change restarts timing; agreement clears it; dedupe is 15 minutes.
- Both are persisted severity-`warning` events. They are not behaviors, danger events, diagnoses, or medical claims.

## Decisions (with rationale)

- Keep the existing C++ contract core and add two real Pico SDK targets instead of replacing tested host behavior.
- Run OpenCV/YOLO on the Windows host, not Pico or Docker.
- Use FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Paho MQTT, OpenCV, Ultralytics YOLO, PostgreSQL, the Sites 0.1.27 vinext starter, and Sites; add no extra service layer.
- Use fake camera/MQTT fixtures in CI and preserve evidence `kind=contract-mock|local-live|sites-production|hardware` separately from `status=PASS|FAIL|NOT_RUN`.
- Require PostgreSQL for local-live and CI integration acceptance; SQLite remains unit-test-only.
- Use Alembic for the nine-table schema: `devices`, `sensor_readings`, `cameras`, `zones`, `camera_events`, `behavior_events`, `anomaly_events`, `bed_calibrations`, and `rest_sessions`.
- Bind FastAPI to `127.0.0.1`, enforce exact Origins, keep Sites owner-only, and reject anonymous MQTT.
- Persist anomaly rows before dashboard WebSocket broadcast; a failed broadcast leaves them available through `/api/anomalies`.
- Never publish the local camera/backend to the internet.
- Keep one stable subject per pet class. First reject nonfinite/reversed detections, clamp float `xyxy` to 640x480, floor left/top, ceil right/bottom, reject zero area, derive the exact integer center, and choose the highest-confidence normalized detection per class over the whole frame; break same-class equal-confidence ties by the lexicographically smallest normalized bbox tuple. Then use exact half-open center membership, reserve positive-area bbox/ROI intersection for calibration on that same selected detection, exact-cross-class-tie-select dog, retain an existing owner while present, and exclude multiple same-class identity tracking.
- Power SHT31/HX711/FSR dividers at 3.3 V with common grounds and 10 kOhm FSR starter resistors; power LD2410C from regulated 5 V >=200 mA and connect only documented 3.3 V UART_TX to Pico RX GP9 at 256000 8N1.
- Keep firmware FSR configuration to ADC pins, cadence, and electrical facts; backend configuration exclusively owns polarity, stability, entry, and exit thresholds.
- Seed only the two exact enabled 640x480 zones; PUT enforces strict bounds and one-transaction non-overlap, allowing edge touch; no create/delete route.
- Use exact Sites identity/access plus the credential/subtree/tree/push/package/save/private-deploy/status/open chain; hosting adds only project_id and the demo makes no environment mutation.
